Five Largest Protests in U.S. History

By Catherine Montesi

  1. The George Floyd Protests are ongoing protests for racial justice and against police voilence.
  2. The 2017 Women's March on Washinton was the largest single-day protest in United States history.
  3. March for Our Lives, the student-led action and it's nearly 900 sibling marches had an estimated turnout of between 1.2 and 2 million protestors.
  4. The 2018 Women's March took place in cities around the world on the one-year anniversary of the 2017 Women's March.
  5. The March on Washington for Lesbian, Gay, and Bi Equal Rights and Liberation drew 1,000,000 people to Washington DC in April of 1993.
